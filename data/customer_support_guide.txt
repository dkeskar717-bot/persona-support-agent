Customer Support Guide

1. Password Reset
Users can reset their password using the "Forgot Password" option.

2. Account Lock
Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts.

3. API Authentication
A 401 Unauthorized error usually indicates an invalid API key.

4. Billing Issues
Billing disputes are reviewed within 5 business days.

5. Refund Policy
Refund requests are processed within 7 business days.

6. Email Verification
Users must verify their email before accessing premium services.

7. Subscription Management
Subscriptions can be upgraded, downgraded, or canceled from account settings.

8. Payment Failure
Payment failures may occur due to insufficient balance or expired cards.

9. Login Problems
Users should check credentials and clear browser cache.

10. Technical Support
For unresolved issues, contact technical support.
